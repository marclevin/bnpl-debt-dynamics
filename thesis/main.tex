\subsubsection*{Research Questions}
This thesis is guided by the following primary research question:

\begin{quote}
    How does the availability of multiple concurrent \ac{BNPL} facilities affect the trajectory of consumer financial distress among low-to-middle income South African households, and what platform-level interventions can reduce systemic default cascades?
\end{quote}

This is decomposed into three subsidiary questions:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Under what conditions does debt stacking become self-reinforcing?
    \item At what threshold of \ac{BNPL} access does the population-level default rate shift non-linearly?
    \item Do ``cool-off period'' interventions meaningfully alter outcomes, or do agents simply defer rather than desist?
\end{enumerate}
